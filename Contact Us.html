Toggle FAQ

Who can join?                                 

Anyone, really. As long as you want to have fun and read a lot. Also, as long as you can be nice, polite and respectful during debates. 

What if I can't join the meeting this month?                                 

That's okay. We know that life is crazy and we can't always be present. Just make sure to let us know in advance, even if it's right before the meeting, so we know you're all right even if you can't join.

Do I need a specific edition?

No, you can read whichever edition you have access too. Also any format is okay as well (electronic or print).

Are all meetings happening in English ?                                 

Unfortunately for people who don't speak English, yes. We have international members as well and we'd like everyone to understand what is being discussed and every member to be able to participate and express themselves as well.
